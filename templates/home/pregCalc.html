Pregnancy Milestones

1st
Trimester

After fertilization and implantation, your baby is just an embryo, that is a set of cells from which all organs and body parts will develop. It will be exciting to see how fast your baby grows. From this minuscule starting point, all the organs and body parts begin their intricate formation. It's a time of anticipation, wonder, and eager anticipation of the journey ahead, as you nurture and support the incredible transformation happening within you.

2nd
Trimester

At the beginning of the second trimester, your baby is about 3 1/2 inches long and weigh about 1 1/2 ounces. Tiny, unique fingerprints are now in place, and the heart pumps 25 quarts of blood a day. As the weeks go by, your baby's skeleton starts to harden from rubbery cartilage to bone, and he or she develops the ability to hear. You're likely to feel kicks and flutters soon if you haven't already.

Milestones

Week 23

3rd
Trimester

As this trimester progresses, your little one reaches around 14-1/2 inches in length and weighs approximately 2 pounds. Throughout these final weeks before birth, your baby's organs and body systems refine and mature, ensuring they are ready for life outside the womb. With each passing day, you may feel the joy of your baby's active movements, as they stretch and kick inside you. The anticipation and excitement build as the third trimester progresses, bringing you closer to the moment when you will finally hold your precious bundle of joy in your arms.
